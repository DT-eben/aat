Welcome to ATT

Where Accra's Energy
Meets Stillness

Tucked behind a canopy of frangipani trees just minutes from the city's pulse, ATT Hotel was built for travellers who want Accra's warmth without its noise. Every room is designed around natural light, hand-finished furniture, and views of our courtyard gardens.

Since opening, we've welcomed diplomats, honeymooners, and families marking milestones — each given the same unhurried attention. Our team is small by design, so that no request goes unanswered.

Our Story

About ATT Hotel

Nine years of quiet hospitality in the heart of Accra.

How We Began

Built on One Idea: Be Remembered

ATT Hotel was founded in 2016 by the Tetteh family, who converted their family compound in Cantonments into Accra's first true boutique hotel. The goal was never to be the biggest — it was to be the one guests talk about years later.

2016
ATT Hotel opens with eight rooms and a single chef in the kitchen.
2019
The Frangipani Restaurant opens to the public, beyond hotel guests.
2022
Garden Pavilion completed, hosting our first hotel wedding.
2025
Eighteen rooms across four categories, with a 4.9 average guest rating.
